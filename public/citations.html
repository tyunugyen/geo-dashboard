🎯 Citation Types — Editorial vs Paid

📋 Editorial Citations
Chosen by editors based on methodology. These are what AI models cite. Getting here requires outreach + product merit. Cannot be paid for directly on NerdWallet, Forbes, FitSmallBusiness. This is the primary target for unaided SOV.
💰 Paid / Affiliate Placements
Sponsored slots, affiliate links, "Compare Quotes" buttons. These generate traffic and leads but AI models do not cite paid placements — they cite editorial content. Track these separately.
